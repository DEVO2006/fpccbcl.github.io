Message from THE SECRETARY

THE TESY JOURNEY SINCE INCEPTION TO DATE

          

A Journey Towards Regional Financial Growth and Industrialization

On April 28, 2023, at around 4 PM, I received a call from Mr. Kasande Paul, the Regional Team Leader of the Private Sector Foundation Uganda. He invited me to a meeting at Bishop Magambo Counsel Institute, Virika. I was on-site when the call came through, but as has always been my habit, I seized the opportunity to contribute where I could be of value. Mr. Kasande was apologetic for the short notice, but I assured him of my commitment to attend in person—and I did.

Upon arrival, a crucial discussion was already underway on how to improve the financial performance of our region. The dialogue was highly structured and productive, leading to the formation of a task force led by Eng. Gilbert Mujogya Atwooki. Our mandate was to develop a TESY Road Map within four working weeks. We successfully delivered this on May 30, 2023, presenting it to Isaazi Lya Abantu Bakuru ba Tooro, where it received much appreciation.

With a clear vision in place, we immediately set to work, focusing on preparations for the regional symposium—a pivotal event aimed at shaping the region’s industrialization efforts.

Laying the Foundation

Our first preparation meeting was held on Friday, May 19, 2023, centering on two key aspects:

  1. District Visits
  2. Review of the Road Map

Since then, we have held over 32 meetings to ensure the success of this significant event. However, beyond the symposium, we resolved to establish a long-term entrepreneurship forum  platform where entrepreneurs can unite, share knowledge, and leverage opportunities for growth.

This forum, still in its formative stage, will serve a key purpose:

  • Organizing producers within their respective sectors
  • Advocating for their needs
  • Training entrepreneurs on best business practices
  • Connecting them to capital, social networks, and other vital production resources

Gratitude to Our Partners

As we stand on the brink of this transformative journey, we extend our deepest gratitude to the partners and individuals who have made this vision possible.

Moving Forward

As we embark on a series of enterprising activities over the next 12 months, we call upon your continued support. Exciting opportunities lie ahead for entrepreneurs, and we are committed to ensuring exponential growth and improvements in their businesses.

Together, we are building a stronger, more resilient economic future for our region. Let us continue this journey hand in hand, driven by vision, collaboration, and unwavering determination.

 

The writer is the Secretary to the TESY Taskforce
