The Fort Portal City Chamber of Business and Commerce (FPCCBC) is a non-profit corporation established in Fort Portal

 Tourism city in the Republic of Uganda. It operates as a business membership organization, providing administrative and organizational support to both local and international entrepreneurs and partners.

Situated at the base of the Rwenzori Mountains in the greater Tooro kingdom, Western Region of the Country. Known for

 being vibrant and welcoming, Fort Portal is the gateway to Uganda's adventures. Fort Portal City lies on the northern

 foothills of Rwenzori Mountain ranges, 50km north of Kasese District close to Queen Elizabeth National Park and it is 26km from Kibale national park.
