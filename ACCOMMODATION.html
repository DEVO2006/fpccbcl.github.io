Best Accommodations in the region

  Sitting on the west side of the country and lying in tthe greater western arm of the east African rift valley, the region is blessed with abundant lakes, rivers,forests and parks lying in the greater national parks giving you an eye taking sceens that can bring comfort mind and heart and also soothing up your heart.

    Moving from the beautiful vast wilderness of the kabale and the mighty elizabeth park, best accomodations were built to provide a heart soothing experience during your visit in the region ranging from the mega 5 star hotels and lodges to the wild camping sites all just made to make you feel at home during your walk of adventure.                                                                                                                                             

1. Kyaninga Lodge

       Kyaninga Lodge offers a luxurious African retreat nestled in an ancient volcanic crater with views of the Rwenzori Mountains. The lodge features nine handcrafted cottages elevated above the

 ground for privacy and tranquility. Guests can choose between twin or double beds, with each cottage equipped with a freestanding bathtub, glass-walled shower (hot and cold water), and

 charging points powered by modern solar energy.

2. Ndali Crater Lodge

      Ndali Crater Lodge is a luxury lodge located outside Kibale Forest National Park, offering stunning views of the serene Ndali Crater Lake. Activities include hiking to Nyinabulitwa Crater Lake,

 guided nature walks, birdwatching, and canoeing or fishing. The lodge is perfect for nature enthusiasts seeking relaxation amidst breathtaking landscapes.

3. Papaya Lake Lodge

      Papaya Lake Lodge is a stylish lodge situated on the rim of Lyantonde Crater Lake, close to Fort Portal town and Kibale National Forest. Surrounded by evergreen vegetation, the lodge

 boasts stunning views of the Rwenzori Mountains, crater lakes, and hills.

The lodge has nine spacious cottages, including single, double, and family options, each with a deck, simple yet comfortable furniture, and en-suite bathrooms. Guests can enjoy a swimming pool, a bar/lounge, a restaurant, and additional services such as massages, laundry, and free Wi-Fi. Activities include chimpanzee tracking, nature walks, crater lake tours, and visits to Mahoma Falls.

4. Primate Lodge Kibale

     Primate Lodge is a mid-range accommodation located in the heart of Kibale Forest, offering an ideal base for wildlife enthusiasts. The lodge features eight safari tents and seven treehouse-

style cottages, blending comfort with a traditional African vibe. Each unit includes an en-suite bathroom, a cozy sitting area, and a veranda for relaxing amidst nature.

      The lodge provides a bar, lounge, and restaurant serving local and international cuisine, along with laundry services and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Guests can enjoy chimpanzee tracking,

 birdwatching, and nature walks.

5. Chimpundu Lodge

      Chimpundu Lodge is a mid-range lodge named after the local word "Mpundu," meaning chimpanzee in the Runyankole-Rukiga language. Located 309 kilometers from Kampala, the lodge

 offers a serene escape within Kibale National Park. Guests can relax in the comfortable lounge, savor meals at the restaurant, or enjoy the swimming pool and satellite TV in private rooms.


Activities include chimpanzee trekking, nature walks, and unwinding in the tranquil surroundings.

6. Kibale Forest Lodge

      Located at the entrance of Kibale Forest National Park, Kibale Forest Lodge is a haven for nature lovers. Surrounded by lush tropical forests, the lodge offers accommodations that blend local

 architectural styles with modern comfort.


Guests can choose between luxury tents and cottages, each featuring thatched roofs, wooden furnishings, en-suite bathrooms, and private verandas. Activities include chimpanzee tracking,

 birdwatching, and guided nature walks, making the lodge a perfect base for exploring the park.

      With its diverse range of accommodations, Kibale Forest National Park ensures a memorable and comfortable stay for all visitors. Whether you prefer luxury, mid-range, or budget options,

the park offers the perfect retreat to experience Uganda's incredible wildlife and natural beauty.
